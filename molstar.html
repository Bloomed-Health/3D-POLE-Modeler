Catalytic mechanism

Two-metal-ion catalysis: Metal A (Mg²+) lowers the pKa of the primer 3′-OH. Metal B coordinates the β,γ-phosphates of the incoming dNTP. Both metals are chelated by conserved aspartates D640, D642, D860.
